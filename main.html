Goa


Goa is a beautiful coastal destination in India known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich culture, and relaxing atmosphere.

Top Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – famous for nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – largest beach in Goa
  3. Fort Aguada – historic Portuguese fort
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – breathtaking natural beauty
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO heritage site

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Bebinca
  • Pork Vindaloo
  • Feni
